Academic Research Experience

IIT Dharwad

Doctoral Student · Sep 2020 – Present

Provisional Thesis: Case-specific 3D models of arbitrarily complex branched topologies for modelling morphology and growth

My research work involves generating geometric models from three-dimensional images obtained from medical scans and assessing the reconstruction accuracy using voxel and surface based accuracy metrics. Statistical nature of surface deviation errors arising due to voxelization of continuous geometries was also explored. Quantifying the smoothing behaviour using Laplace-Beltrami spectral analysis and morphological analysis of tubular geometries form the other research domains my work spans across.

Supervisor: Dr. Samarth S. Raut, Assistant Professor, IIT Dharwad

RV College of Engineering, Bengaluru

Master Student · July 2016 – July 2018

Thesis: Numerical study of induction hardening on rocker arm shaft using STARCCM+ and DEFORM

This works involves the simulation of the induction hardening process - Heat transfer simulation using CFD tool STARCCM+, followed by CAM tool DEFORM to compute residual stresses due to quenching.

Supervisor: Dr. Nataraj J R, Associate Professor, RVCE

National Institute of Engineering, Mysuru

Bachelor Student · Sep 2010 – July 2014

Thesis: Design of control system to delay stall using active flow control · CTFD, NAL-CSIR

This work is a four-module project: CFD validation on NACA0012, inclusion of synthetic jets for active flow control, simulation of piezo-resistive sensor arrays, and control system design to optimise synthetic jet parameters.

Supervisor: Dr. T. N. Shridhar, Professor, NIE Mysuru
